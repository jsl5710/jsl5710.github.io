My paper was accepted at the 2026 ACL Main Conference. This research is titled, [DIA-HARM: Dialectal Disparities in Harmful Content Detection Across 50 English Dialects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05318). DIA-HARM is the first benchmark for evaluating disinformation detection robustness across 50 English dialects spanning U.S., British, African, Caribbean, and Asia-Pacific varieties, with the **D3** corpus of 195K samples and an evaluation of 16 detection models. This work is a collaboration with Penn State, MIT Lincoln Laboratory, and the University of Dublin. Coauthors include — Matt Murtagh, Ali Al-Lawati, Uchendu Uchendu, Adaku Uchendu & Dongwon Lee.
